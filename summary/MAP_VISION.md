# Singapore Sea Transport Vision → Nexus RM&D Integration

This document summarizes the **Sea Transport Industry Transformation Map (ITM)**, developed by the **Maritime and Port Authority (MPA)** of Singapore, and maps its key priorities to the **Nexus RM&D Platform** capabilities.

📄 Source: [Sea Transport Industry Transformation Map 2025](https://www.mpa.gov.sg/maritime-singapore/what-maritime-singapore-offers/industry-transformation)

---

## 1. The Vision: Sea Transport Industry Transformation Map (Refreshed 2022)

The Sea Transport ITM lay out the blueprint to develop Singapore’s next-generation port and strengthen the International Maritime Centre (IMC). It addresses challenges such as global supply chain shifts, digital disruption, and the green transition.

### Five Pillars of the ITM

| Pillar | Focus Area | Relevance to Nexus RM&D |
|--------|------------|-------------------------|
| **Innovation** | Vibrant Ecosystem, MPA Living Lab, Port Innovation Ecosystem Reimagined (PIER71) | Open platform for maritime R&D, third-party analytics, and start-up solutions. |
| **Productivity** | Automated & Digital Environment, Sea Transport Industry Digital Plan (IDP) | Remote monitoring of port equipment, automated diagnostic reporting, and digitalization of SME maritime vendors. |
| **Jobs and Skills** | Future-Ready Maritime Workforce, Skills Framework for Sea Transport | Data-driven decision support tools, reskilling maritime engineers into data-centric roles. |
| **Internationalisation** | Growth into Global Champions | Scalable, cloud-native architecture enabling Singapore maritime companies to manage global fleets. |
| **Resilience** | Port Capacity, Capabilities & Connectivity | High-availability telemetry, predictive maintenance for critical port infrastructure to ensure global supply chain stability. |

---

## 2. Vision → Nexus Implementation Mapping

### Innovation Thrust (MPA Living Lab / PIER71)
Nexus provides the **standardized telemetry envelope** and **plugin API** that allows maritime technology enterprises and start-ups to deploy innovative solutions (e.g., fuel efficiency monitoring, automated berthing analytics, hull structural health) without reinventing data ingestion infrastructure.

### Productivity Thrust (Highly Automated Maritime Environment)
Nexus's **automated KPI reporting** and **real-time alerting** directly support MPA's goal of enhancing operational efficiencies. By integrating with the **Sea Transport IDP** (Industry Digital Plan), Nexus makes "going digital simple" for maritime SMEs.

### Resilience Thrust ( catch-up port / Essential Nexus)
Nexus's **Remote Monitoring & Diagnostics (RM&D)** is a critical capability for ensuring port infrastructure (cranes, AGVs, berthing systems) remains operational 24/7. Predictive maintenance reduces unplanned downtime, bolstering Singapore’s reputation as a resilient "Global Hub Port."

## 4. Glossary (Key Maritime Terms)

- **MPA:** Maritime and Port Authority of Singapore.
- **IMC:** International Maritime Centre.
- **ITM:** Industry Transformation Map.
- **IDP:** Industry Digital Plan (specifically for Sea Transport SMEs).
- **PIER71:** Port Innovation Ecosystem Reimagined @ BLOCK71.
- **LIVING LAB:** MPA’s environment for creating and testing innovative maritime solutions.
