# Singapore Environmental Services Vision → Nexus RM&D Integration

This document summarizes the **Environmental Services Industry Transformation Map (ES ITM)**, led by the **National Environment Agency (NEA)**, and maps its priorities to the **Nexus RM&D Platform**.

📄 Source: [Environmental Services Industry Transformation Map 2025](https://www.nea.gov.sg/our-services/environmental-services-industry-transformation-map)

---

## 1. The Vision: Environmental Services ITM (Refreshed 2025)

The ES ITM covers the **Cleaning**, **Waste Management**, and **Pest Management** sectors. It aims to create stronger businesses and better jobs through automation, digitalization, and greener growth.

### Four Key Thrusts of the ES ITM

| Thrust | Focus Area | Relevance to Nexus RM&D |
|--------|------------|-------------------------|
| **Innovation** | High-tech solutions for cleaning & waste management. | Integration of "Smart Bins" (fill sensors) and "Smart Pest Sensors" into a unified RM&D dashboard. |
| **Productivity** | Accelerating digitalization and automation. | Demand-based cleaning (triggered by sensor telemetry) instead of schedule-based routes. |
| **Quality Jobs** | Reskilling and upskilling for a more technical workforce. | Training traditional supervisors to use RM&D tools for remote workforce and asset management. |
| **Sustainability** | Green growth and environmental resilience. | Monitoring energy/water consumption of cleaning facilities and optimizing waste collection fuel efficiency. |

---

## 2. Vision → Nexus Implementation Mapping

### Demand-Based Operations (Productivity)
Nexus enables the transition from schedule-based to **demand-based services**. By ingesting telemetry from occupancy sensors (in toilets) or fill-level sensors (in bins), the platform can generate real-time "cleaning alerts" or "collection required" signals, significantly increasing manpower productivity in the Cleaning and Waste sectors.

### Robotic Integration (Innovation)
Building on **TR93** and **SS713** (Robot-Infrastructure standards), Nexus provides the gateway for **Cleaning Robots (AMRs)** to interact with building infrastructure (lifts, doors) and report their operational health. This allows for unified monitoring of "Human + Robot" cleaning fleets.

### Sector Resilience
The ES ITM 2025 emphasizes resilience. Nexus's **Remote Monitoring & Diagnostics** allows essential services (waste management) to maintain high uptime with leaner teams by using predictive maintenance for compactors, sorting machines, and collection vehicles.

## 4. Glossary (Key Environmental Terms)

- **NEA:** National Environment Agency.
- **ES ITM:** Environmental Services Industry Transformation Map.
- **EMAS:** Environmental Management Association of Singapore.
- **WMAS:** Waste Management and Recycling Association of Singapore.
- **Demand-Based Cleaning:** A workflow where cleaning is performed based on actual usage/data rather than a fixed schedule.
